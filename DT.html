Play or Lie

A thrilling game of deception and strategy. Can you uncover the truth?

GAME INSTRUCTIONS

How to Play:

  • Each player draws a card from the deck.
  • On your turn, place a card and claim its identity.
  • Your opponent must decide if you are **lying or telling the truth.**
  • If they call your bluff correctly, you lose the round! ❌
  • If they guess wrong, they lose! ✅
